Glory Oath + Blood is an epic music band from Los Angeles. The group was founded in 2014 by film composer Robert Leslie Bennett as a collaborative outlet to experiment in pushing the boundaries of the epic music genre. Group members include Thor Laewe, Danny McCarthy, Tobias Enhus, and Charlie Lin. Their albums are distributed by Warner/Chappell Production Music. All Glory Oath + Blood albums feature the artwork of Devon Gibbs.
